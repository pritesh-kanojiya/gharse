Our Story

Ghar Se Cafe is a humble startup by Chef Sunita, who has been baking professionally since 2015. Over the past decade, she has learned from multiple master chefs and mastered the art of creating exotic bakery delights from around the world, including cuisines from countries like France, Germany, Japan, Mexico, and our beloved India.

Chef Sunita still continues to pursue her passion for perfection, learning from renowned online institutions and master chefs while experimenting with her own unique sense of flavors.

With a decade of baking mastery, she is now ready to share the fruits of her dedication with the people of Nashik.
